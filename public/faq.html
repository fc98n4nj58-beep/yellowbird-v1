FAQ

Simple answers now. We’ll expand this as downloads, accounts, and licensing evolve.

Do I need an account?

No. Phase 1 is instant downloads. Accounts come later for favorites + history.

Are resources Ontario-aligned?

Ontario first, global later. Each resource will show alignment info as we tag the library.

What file formats are available?

PDF now. Google Slides/Docs and other formats are supported as the catalog grows.
